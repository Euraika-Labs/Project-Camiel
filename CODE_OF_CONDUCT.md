# Code of Conduct

Project Camiel should be a kind, safe, and constructive space.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and patient.
- Assume good intent while still naming problems clearly.
- Keep feedback focused on the work.
- Be mindful that this project is intended for very young children.
- Welcome contributors with different backgrounds and experience levels.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or demeaning comments.
- Sexualized language or imagery.
- Discrimination or exclusionary behavior.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated disruption after maintainers ask for a change in behavior.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, restrict participation, or take other reasonable action to protect the project community.

If you see a problem, report it privately to the maintainers through the repository owner or GitHub's reporting tools.
